rotis or flatbreads
roti is part of almost every meal in the region and is delicious when eaten hot and fresh off the stove. people in haryana are fond of eating various types of rotis: from wheat rotis to baajre ki roti (flatbread of pearl millets). there are other varieties too that are made of gram, barley etc., that are truly nutritious.
