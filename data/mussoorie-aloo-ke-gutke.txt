aloo ke gutke
this pahari aloo recipe is the regional culinary gem of uttarakhand that is simple and flavourful. it is prepared using boiled pahari potato, red chilli powder, salt, turmeric, coriander leaves and coriander powder. it is mildly spiced and commonly eaten during festivals. one can find this dish at local restaurants too. it is best eaten with puris and raita.
