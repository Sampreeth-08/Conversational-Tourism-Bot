gokarna
Travel Air Road Train
by air  the nearest airport is at dabolim, goa, about 140 km away. the airport is well-linked to both domestic and international cities.
by road  gokarna is easily accessible by good motorable roads and regular buses ply to gokarna, which is well-connected to other major cities of the country.
rail  gokarna railway station is part of the konkan railway system but not all trains stop here. the nearest well-connected railhead is at ankhola, about 20 km away.
