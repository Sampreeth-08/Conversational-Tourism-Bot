jodhpur
Travel Air Road Train
by air  jodhpur domestic airport is 5 km away from the city centre. the city being a prime tourist spot is connected by all private and national air carriers.
by road  jodhpur is well-connected to major cities of india by road. the highway has good connectivity from jaipur, delhi, jaisalmer, bikaner and agra.
rail  the city is well connected by the railway lines and trains are available from all metro cities.
