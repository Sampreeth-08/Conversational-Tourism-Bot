all the way upto janavpav kuti
the janapav kuti peak is the highest point of the malwa plateau and is also famous as the birthplace of lord parshurama, an incarnation of lord vishnu. the climb to the top of the plateau requires one to walk through a dense forest. stop at the ashram dedicated to jamadagni, lord parshurama's father, who used to live here with his wife, renuka. legend has it that the variety of medicinal herbs found in the area were grown by her. the janapav kuti peak offers splendid views of the lush green surroundings and the incredible scenic skyline of indore. it is situated at a distance of 45 km from indore and is easily reachable.
