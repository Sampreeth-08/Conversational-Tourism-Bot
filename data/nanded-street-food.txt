food-and-cuisine
street food
the vibrant street food culture of nanded has something to offer to everyone. it includes north indian staples like samosas (savoury pastry fried in oil), aloo tikki (fried and spiced potato), bhelpuri (a tangy snack of puffed rice), paprichaat (snack made with crispy poori and topped with yoghurt and chutney) and fruit salads. it also offers more cosmopolitan options like chinese, mughlai, tandoori cuisines.
