orchha
Travel Air Road Train
by air  the closest airport to orchha is located in gwalior, about 123 km away. gwalior is connected to new delhi by direct flight service.
by road since orchha falls on the jhansi-khajuraho route, the main bus stop is at jhansi. several state-run buses ply on this route.
rail jhansi in uttar pradesh is the nearest railway station. it is connected to cities like new delhi and bhopal by regular train service. the distance between jhansi and orchha is about 17 km.
