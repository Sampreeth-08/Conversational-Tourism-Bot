vengurla
lying at a distance of 175 km from kolhapur and 110 km from goa, vengurla is a prominent stopover on the tourist circuit. it is best known for its long serene coastline and shore temples. the main attraction is the crescent-shaped vengurla beach that boasts a blanket of sun-kissed golden sand. bordered by hills of rolling green dotted with mango, cashew and coconut trees, the beach is a delight for vacationers, who can soak in abundant natural beauty.
