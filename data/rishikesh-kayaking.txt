kayaking
a kayak is a long boat with double seaters and paddles on both sides. it has double blade paddles that help in negotiating the waves and steering the boat. river ganga in rishikesh offers a number of challenging rapids for good kayaking opportunities. beginners can choose rapids that fit their skill and meet their comfort level. while kayaking, one an explore the himalayan caves and the high mountain waterfalls along with the cooling waters of the ganges.
