tirupati
Travel Air Road Train
by air the tirupati airport in renigunta has frequent flights to major indian cities such as chennai, hyderabad and bengaluru.
by road there are frequent buses that run from major cities here. the government buses in the region link this region with every part of andhra pradesh and karnataka.
rail the tirupati railway station is linked to major stations of bengaluru, chennai, visakhapatnam, madurai and mumbai. renigunta rail station is very close to the city and is at a distance of 15 km.
