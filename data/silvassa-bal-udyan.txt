bal udyan
one of the landmarks of silvassa, bal udyan is a children's park that attracts tourists with its peaceful environment. the park is widely popular among kids and its prime attractions include a mini zoo and an aquarium. while the mini zoo is home to a number of animals, birds and reptiles, including pythons and crocodiles, the aquarium houses tropical fish and opens at 4 pm. the park has many fun rides for children including the evergreen see-saw, merry-go-round and other swings. bal udyan is located right next to the circuit house and is definitely a must-visit for every tourist travelling to silvassa.
