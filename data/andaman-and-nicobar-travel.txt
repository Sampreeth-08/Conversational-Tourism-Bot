andaman-and-nicobar
Travel Air Road Train
by air  direct flights to port blair, the capital, are available from all metro cities of india. from there, travellers can take ships that are available on a regular basis to reach the andamans.
by road  andaman express passes through major cities like new delhi and chennai.
sea three to four ships sail from kolkata and chennai every month, bound for port blair and vice versa. ships also sail from visakhapatnam, but are less frequent.
