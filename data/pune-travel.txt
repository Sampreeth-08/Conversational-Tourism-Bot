pune
Travel Air Road Train
by air  pune has its own international airport at lohegaon and there are direct flights from most major cities of the country.
by road pune has motorable roads and highways in good condition. they are connected to all the major indian towns and cities including ahmednagar, aurangabad, kolhapur and mumbai by several state and roadways buses. from mumbai, the 150 km mumbai-pune expressway takes you around three hours to reach your destination.
rail pune is a major railhead and connected to all the major indian cities. from mumbai, the deccan queen and shatabdi express take you to pune in around four hours.
