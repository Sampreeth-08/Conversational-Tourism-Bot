chaat
spicy, crunchy, tangy and sour-sweet chaat can be relished in ayodhya. the delicacies include pani puri, a crisp fried puri served with tamarind water; kachori, fried and spicy dumpling; aloo tikki, a preparation of boiled potatoes, peas and various spices; papri chaat, a tangy snack served with crisp fried wafers with a topping of yoghurt, tamarind and sev; samosa, fried puffed pastry eaten with chutney.
