daman
Travel Air Road Train
by air  situated at about 170 km, the mumbai airport is the nearest airport from daman which connects all major cities of india.
by road daman is connected to other cities by mumbai-ahmedabad highway via vapi road. there are regular bus services from major cities, including mumbai, surat, ahmedabad, udaipur, nasik and shirdi.
rail the nearest rail head to daman is vapi in gujarat, which is about seven km away. the rail head is well-connected to other cities through ahmedabad and mumbai.
