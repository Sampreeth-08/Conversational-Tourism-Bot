silvassa 
bindrabin-temple 
often referred to as tadkeshwara temple by locals, bindrabin temple is one of the most visited spots near silvassa. located on the banks of river sakartod, it is dedicated to lord shiva and holds special significance in the culture and heritage of silvassa. set amidst a picture perfect landscape, the temple is surrounded by tall trees. it is also noted for its intricate wall carvings that reveal indian tradition and culture. the temple is known for hosting cultural programmes during which the locals perform dances about the folklore associated with the temple. the temple complex houses three cottages, a recreational area and an eatery for visitors. the temple lies at a distance of 18 km from silvassa and 2 km from khanvel.
