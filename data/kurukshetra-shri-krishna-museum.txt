shri krishna museum
kurukshetra has a museum dedicated to lord krishna, who is at the heart of the ethos and identity of the city. one can find exhibits related to the various events in the epic mahabharata and the gita involving lord krishna. the museum has six galleries, three in each of its two blocks. on display are stone sculptures, bronze castings, leaf etchings, miniature paintings, clay pottery and terracotta artefacts. haryana tourism, in collaboration with the ministry of tourism, government of india, has also set up a multimedia mahabharata and gita gallery that are a must visit here. shri krishna museum is located right next to the kurukshetra panorama and science centre.
