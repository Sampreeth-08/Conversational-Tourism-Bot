tashi-jong 
located in the quaint village of tashi jong, the tashi jong monastery is a vibrant landmark surrounded by picturesque snow-capped mountains and lush landscape. it is a centre of the drugpa kagyu tradition of buddhism and the complex comprises a temple, the yamantak retreat centre that remains closed to the public and the  stupa of khamtrul rinpoche, a lineage of buddhism. the building has been designed by him and is a gorgeous structure adorned with intricate wood carvings and beautiful paintings. it also houses several manifestations of lord buddha, the most popular being buddha shakyamuni, a traditional representation of the ancient buddha. the monastery is also a crafts emporium from where tourists can shop for tibetan handicrafts. tashi jong in tibetan stands for auspicious valley.
