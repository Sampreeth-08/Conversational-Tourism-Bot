ahmedabad
Travel Air Road Train
by air the sardar vallabhbhai international airport is at a 10 km distance from the periphery of ahmedabad city. one can take a cab from there.
by road well-constructed roads and motorable highways connect ahmedabad to the rest of india. bus, taxis, cars on rent, are all available.
rail  the ahmedabad railway junction is the biggest railway station in gujarat and is well-connected to most of india.
