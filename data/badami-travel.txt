badami
Travel Air Road Train
by air hubli (106 km) and belgaum, around 150 km away, are the nearest airports to badami.
by road  the town is connected by good motorable roads to bijapur and hubli. it takes around 12 hours between bengaluru and badami by road.
rail  badami falls on hubli-solapur rail route. the station is 5 km from badami.
