ghoghla beach
situated in the village ghoghla, on the outskirts of diu, this lesser explored beach is popular for water sports opportunities. tourists can take a water scooter ride in the sea or try parasailing. a long stretch of golden sand, ghoghla beach is the largest in diu and is famous for bewitching views of sunrises and sunsets. you can also drink in the surreal beauty of the landscape while lazing around on the beach. tourists can enjoy banana boat rides and surfing at this relatively clean beach.
