darjeeling 
iskcon-chandrodaya-temple 
constructed under lord mahaprabhu, this temple is dedicated to lord krishna. it has a section displaying the life and works of shree chaitanya. the sanctum sanctorum houses an elaborately ornamented idol of lord krishna. 
 
one of the most important shrines for hindus in west bengal, iskcon temple is highly popular for its krishna janmashtami celebrations, usually taking place in august of every year. crowded with devotees all year round, iskcon is abuzz with activity and spiritual energy, allowing you to immerse yourself in the prayers and the chants that ring here throughout the day. 
 
the surroundings are quite peaceful, offering great views of the surrounding mountains. you can sit and meditate, or just enjoy the beauty of the surroundings at this lovely temple.
