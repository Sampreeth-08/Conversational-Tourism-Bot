handmade paper
while visiting puducherry, one should definitely look out for eco-friendly handmade paper that is quite popular. made for over half a century by sri aurobindo handmade paper factory, it comes in various colours. one can buy other stationery items and gift boxes along with it. another interesting feature is a tour through the paper-making factory, where one can get a close look at the process and the technique of how paper is produced from cotton rags and then processed with silkscreen printing and marbling. other articles made of handmade paper that one can shop for are photo frames, lanterns and lampshades.
