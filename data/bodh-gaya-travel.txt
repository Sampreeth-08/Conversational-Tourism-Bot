bodh-gaya
Travel Air Road Train
by air  gaya is the nearest airport, approximately 17 km from bodh gaya. the other airport is at state capital patna (135 km away) and has better connectivity.
by road  a main road connects the town of bodh gaya to the city of gaya. you can hire cabs or take a bus.
rail  the nearest rail head is gaya junction, which is 13 km away from bodh gaya.
