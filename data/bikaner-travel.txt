bikaner
Travel Air Road Train
by air the nearest airport is civil airport at jodhpur, which is 253 km away.
by road the bus stand is north of the city centre. there are frequent express buses to agra, ahmedabad, ajmer, barmer, delhi, jaipur, jaisalmer, jhunjhunu, jodhpur, kota and udaipur.
rail bikaner has railway connections to several destinations in india including jaipur, jaisalmer, churu, jodhpur, delhi, mumbai, kolkata, guwahati etc.
