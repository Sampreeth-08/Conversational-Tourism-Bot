yamunotri
Travel Air Road Train
by air  the nearest airport is jolly grant airport in dehradun, which is about 200 km from uttarkashi. taxi and bus services are available from dehradun airport to barkot, uttarkashi.
by road state transport buses traverse regularly between uttarkashi/ barkot and dehradun/ rishikesh. yamunotri is about 130 km away from uttarkashi headquarters.
rail railway stations are there at haridwar, dehradun and rishikesh. dehradun is nearest to yamunotri.
