nashik
Travel Air Road Train
by air the nearest airport, ozar, is located 24 km from the centre of the city.
by road  nashik is well-connected to pune by nh-50. the national highway of mumbai agra passes via nashik and the city is well-connected with all the major cities.
rail  the railway station at nashik serves as an important station from mumbai to kalyan, manmad to bhusaval and to kolkata or delhi. one more station is at deomali, which has as many as 50 trains passing through it. it is well-connected to cities like hyderabad, agra, bhopal, nagpur, jammu, kolkata etc.
