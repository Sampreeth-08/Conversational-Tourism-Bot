baidyanathdham-deoghar
Travel Air Road Train
by air  the nearest airport is at bodh gaya, about 175 km from deogarh. it is known as the loknayak jai prakash narayan international airport.
by road  deogarh is connected to its nearby cities with good motorable roads. regular bus services ply to and fro from deogarh from places like ranchi, patna etc.
rail  deogarh is well-connected to major indian cities and baidyanath dham junction is the major railhead in the city. regular train services are available at this station.
