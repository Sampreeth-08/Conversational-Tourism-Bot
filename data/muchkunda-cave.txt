junagadh 
muchkunda-cave 
situated to the west of damodar temple, near revati kund, is muchkunda cave. it is said to be associated with the legend of muchkund, who had reduced the enemy of lord krishna, kal yavan, to ashes. the cave has a temple dedicated to lord krishna and a shivling that is said to have been established by muchkund. legend has it that lord krishna was escaping from his enemy, kal yavan, and happened upon the cave where muchkand was meditating. the lord threw his blanket over him and hid in a corner. kal yavan followed the lord into the cave, saw the blanket and drew it away. this disturbed muchkunda's meditation and when he saw the demon, a beam of fire came from his eyes and burnt kal yavan to ashes.
