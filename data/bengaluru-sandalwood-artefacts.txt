sandalwood artefacts
karnataka is fringed by a lush forest-belt lined with sandalwood trees. sandalwood is extravagantly used to craft beautiful articles Â like paper-cutters, trays, boxes, fans, walking sticks and idols of deities. other popular items include cigarette cases, holders and photo frames. of these, the most famous one is the figure of an elephant.
these exquisite artefacts can be purchased from any handicraft store in bengaluru. two types of sandalwood are available in the region. these include srigandha, which is close grained and yellowish-brown in colour and is mostly used in carving, and the darkish-brown nagagandha, from which oil is extracted.
