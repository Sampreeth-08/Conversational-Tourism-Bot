munnar
Travel Air Road Train
by air  the nearest airport is in cochin, about 110 km away. one can easily avail cab services from there.
by road  munnar has good, motorable roads connecting it to various cities and points in south india.
rail  the nearest railway stations are aluva and ernakulum, 110 and 125 km away respectively. ernakulum is well-connected to the rest of network in india.
