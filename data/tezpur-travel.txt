Travel Air Road Train
by air the nearest airport, saloni airport, is located around 15 km from tezpur. several flights connect saloni airport with the state capital.
by road roadways is the preferred and most convenient way of travelling to and from tezpur.
rail tezpur railway station called rangapara north railway station has a connect with guwahati railway station. the nearest railhead is guwahati that has trains from all important cities.
