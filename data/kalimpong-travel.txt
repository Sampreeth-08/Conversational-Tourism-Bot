kalimpong
Travel Air Road Train
by air  bagdogra, around 80 km from kalimpong, is the nearest airport and has regular connections with delhi, kolkata and guwahati.
by road  the city of kalimpong is well-connected with all towns and cities in the state and across india.
rail  the nearest railhead is new jalpaiguri which is well-connected by road with siliguri, gangtok and kolkata.
