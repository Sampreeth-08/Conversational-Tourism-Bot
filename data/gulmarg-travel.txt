gulmarg
Travel Air Road Train
by air the nearest airport is located in srinagar, about 57 km from gulmarg. regular flights connect srinagar with the other major cities in india.
by road gulmarg is well connected by road to srinagar, pahalgam, sonamarg, jammu and various other cities of jammu and kashmir.
rail  the nearest railway station is located in jammu, which is connected to other cities of india.
