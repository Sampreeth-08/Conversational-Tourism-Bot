by air tiruchirapalli international airport is at a distance of 60 km from thanjavur. one can take a cab from there.
by road  well-constructed roads and motorable highways connect thanjavur to most of south india. bus, taxis, cars are available on rent.
rail thanjavur is directly connected by rail to cities of tiruchirapalli, chennai, madurai and other south indian cities.
