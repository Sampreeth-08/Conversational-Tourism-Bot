lower bazaar
this market lies below the mall road and is comparatively much cheaper. one can find a lot of silversmith shops from where exquisite silver jewellery items can be purchased. the numerous lanes of the bazaar are lined with shops selling handicrafts, wooden items, colourful earthen pots, shawls and other woollen wear. the market is also known for rare medicinal herbs that can be bought from here.
