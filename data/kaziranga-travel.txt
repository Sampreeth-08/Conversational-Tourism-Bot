kaziranga
Travel Air Road Train
by air rowriah airport in jorhat is the nearest airport, around 50 km away and only operates domestic flights.
by road  the nearest bus stand is at numaligar, around 30 km away and there are no bus routes to kaziranga from other major cities in the state.
rail  furkating, around 75 away, is the nearest railway station. it is connected from guwahati by a number of trains.
