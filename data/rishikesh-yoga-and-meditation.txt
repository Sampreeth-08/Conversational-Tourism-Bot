yoga and meditation
rishikesh hosts a number of ashrams, which are ideal spots to learn and practice yoga. it is popular all over the world as a fine yoga destination. the renowned yoga festival, international yoga festival, is also held in rishikesh, from march 1 to march 7, and is attended by people from all over the world. among the many famous yoga ashrams here, some are parmarth niketan, where the international yoga festival has been held; sivananda ashram also known as divine life society, which provides education on yoga, hinduism and vedanta; omkarananda ganga sadan, where one can get knowledge of spiritual, cultural, educational, scientific and social fields, apart from practising yoga.
