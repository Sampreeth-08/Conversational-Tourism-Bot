nanded
Travel Air Road Train
by air  the nearest airstrip to nanded is in aurangabad, about 253 km.
by road nanded is accessibly by good motorable roads from most places in maharashtra. nanded is 650 km east of mumbai by road and 250 km from hyderabad. it is a ride of about 4-5 hours from aurangabad and 11 hours from pune. several passenger bus services operate out of nanded connecting most major cities through overnight journeys.
rail  nanded is connected with most of the cities in the country through the nanded railway station. sachkhand express is a special super fast train running directly from amritsar to nanded.
