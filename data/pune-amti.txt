amti
with jaggery, tamarind pulp, goda masala (a sweet spice), scraped coconut, cumin seeds, onions, green chillies, asafoetida, fresh coriander and salt, this maharashtrian version of tur dal (split pigeon peas) provides a burst of flavour in the mouth. sweet, spicy and savoury come together to give a rare flavour.
