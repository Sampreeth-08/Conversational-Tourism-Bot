mussoorie
Travel Air Road Train
by air  the nearest airport is the jolly grant airport in dehradun, which lies approximately 54 km away.
by road mussoorie is well-linked with a network of bus services, which includes private tour operators as well as state transport corporations.
rail there are numerous trains connecting new delhi with dehradun, such as the shatabdi express, the mussoorie express, and the nizamuddin ac special.
