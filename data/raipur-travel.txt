raipur
Travel Air Road Train
by air  the main airport connecting raipur to other major cities of the country is the swami vivekananda airport also known as mana airport. it is located near naya raipur, 15 km from the main city. regular flights are available to and from all major indian cities.
by road  buses run regularly to and from raipur to sambalpur district of odisha and nagpur, maharashtra. the nh (national highway) 6 that connects kolkata to mumbai, counts raipur as one of the major cities.
rail  raipur junction railway station lies on the way of a major railway tract of the country, the howrah-nagpur-mumbai line.
