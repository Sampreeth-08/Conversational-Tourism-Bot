Travel Air Road Train
by air  jolly grant airport is the nearest airport to rishikesh, situated at a distance of 30 km. taxis are easily available from jolly grant airport to rishikesh. jolly grant airport is well connected to delhi with daily flights.
by road  rishikesh is well connected by motorable roads with major towns in uttarakhand and other northern states of india. luxurious and regular buses to rishikesh are easily available from delhi, which is 228 km away. it is 18 km away from the sacred town of haridwar.
rail  rishikesh is well connected by railway networks with major destinations of india. trains to rishikesh railway station are frequent.
