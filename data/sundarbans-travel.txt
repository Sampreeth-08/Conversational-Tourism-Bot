sundarbans
Travel Air Road Train
by air  the nearest airport to the sunderbans national park is netaji subhash international airport at dumdum, kolkata, which is 165 km away from the national park.
by road sunderbans is well-connected to its neighbouring areas and kolkata is at a distance of about 110 km from here.
rail the nearest railway station link is canning. it is approximately 48 km away from the sunderbans national park.
