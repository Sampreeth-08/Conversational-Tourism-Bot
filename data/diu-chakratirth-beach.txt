chakratirth beach
this beautiful rocky beach is one of the lesser explored beaches of diu. apart from being serene and blissful, the chakratirth beach also holds mythological significance. legend has it that it was the same place where lord krishna beheaded the demon jalandhar. the perfect way to make the most of this small beach is to just sit and feel the good vibes coming in through cool breezes of the sea. the beach is surrounded by picturesque hills and is a perfect place for taking a peaceful sun bath. an open air auditorium housing changing rooms for tourists has also been set up at the beach. the beautifully maintained gardens around the beach add to its beauty and serenity.
