malvan
lying about 160 km from kolhapur, malvan is a picturesque coastal town that invites tourists from far and wide. it is best known for a marine sanctuary that is sprawled over an area of 30 sq km. one can find rich coral and marine life in the area. for nature-lovers, the beautiful coastline of malvan, along with golden sands and casuarinas plantations give a good opportunity to soak in natural beauty. one can also indulge in various water sports like scuba diving and snorkeling. views of sunsets are particularly spectacular from the beaches.
