hyderabad
Travel Air Road Train
by air the rajiv gandhi international airport in hyderabad is connected to all major indian cities including new delhi, mumbai, bengaluru, pune, and chennai.
by road hyderabad is connected by motorable roads to the neighbouring cities of bangalore, mumbai, kolkata, aurangabad, chennai, tirupati and panaji.
rail three railway stations service the city of hyderabad. these are the hyderabad (nampally station), secunderabad and kachiguda railway stations.
