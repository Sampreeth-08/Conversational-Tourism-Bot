kurukshetra
Travel Air Road Train
by air  the nearest airport is in chandigarh, which is connected with all the metro cities in the country.
by road located on the linking road from nh 1, kurukshetra is well-connected with all the nearby cities.
rail kurukshetra is in itself an important station in the railway network. some of the important trains that pass from here include the shatabdi express, amritsar express, jhelum express, kalka mail and himalayan queen.
