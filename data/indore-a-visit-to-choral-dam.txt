a visit to choral dam
constructed on the backwaters of river narmada, the choral dam is one of the best kept secrets of madhya pradesh that makes for a perfect picnic spot. the dam attracts nature lovers and water sports enthusiasts with its serene surroundings and tranquil vibes. sunrises and sunsets are quite beautiful at the dam when different hues of the sky make for an amazing backdrop for the lush green trees. visitors can also enjoy a ride in a speed or paddle boat between 9 am and 6 pm everyday. the dam is mainly used for irrigation as well as drinking purposes but still serves as a great place to unwind in the lap of nature. the choral dam is located at a distance of 45 km from indore and can be easily reached from other parts of the state as well.
