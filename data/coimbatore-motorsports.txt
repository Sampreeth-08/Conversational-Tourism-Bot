motorsports
coimbatore is often called the hub of motorsports owing to its glorious racing days in the 50s and 60s. the races were mostly held at the sulur airstrip. the last race held here was in september 1967. adrenaline seekers can spend hours experiencing thrill at various motorsport outlets and companies strewn across the city. one can also visit the kari motor speedway and enjoy exciting car races. it also hosts a number of bike races, formula racing events and national championships.
