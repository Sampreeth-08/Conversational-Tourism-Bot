shimla
Travel Air Road Train
by air  the airport, located in jubbarhatii, is about 23 km away from shimla. daily flights connect it to new delhi and kullu.
by road  by bus, shimla is connected to kalka (about 88 km), chandigarh (about 113 km) and new delhi (about 360 km).
rail  broad gauge trains take visitors up till kalka, which is connected by regular rail services to new delhi, kolkata and amritsar. from kalka, one can take narrow gauge trains to shimla using the kalka-shimla unesco world heritage site-listed railway line.
