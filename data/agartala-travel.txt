agartala
Travel Air Road Train
by air  agartala airport is 12 km away from the city and is well connected by air with kolkata, guwahati, delhi and chennai.
by road agartala is connected to major junctions like guwahati, njp, sealdah and anand vihar in new delhi through express trains. the main junction of the state is the one at badharghat in agartala.
rail  connected by nh-44, one can reach agartala from guwahati in 24 hours.
