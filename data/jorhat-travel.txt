jorhat
Travel Air Road Train
by air  jorhat airport is located at rowriah and is approximately 7 km from the city centre. the airport has flights connected to various cities delhi, kolkata, guwahati etc.
by road jorhat is well connected to the roads of other north eastern cities and nh37 connects it with other major cities.
rail there are regular trains from major cities of the country to jorhat town (jttn) and rowriah sdg (rwh).
