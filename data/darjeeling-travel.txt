darjeeling
Travel Air Road Train
by air the nearest airport is at bagdogra that is around 88 km away from darjeeling. it is connected to all the major cities.
by road  the main access to darjeeling is through siliguri, located at about 80 km, which connects to all the major cities of india.
rail  the nearest railway station is the new jalpaiguri (njpa), which connects all the major cities of the country. most of the trains heading the north east stop here.
