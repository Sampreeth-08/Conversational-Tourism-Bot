kausani
Travel Air Road Train
by air the nearest convenient airport is pantnagar, which is 167 km away.
by road  there is good connectivity by road through cars and buses. the nearest cities from where one can drive to kausani include delhi, ranikhet, nainital, dehradun and chandigarh.
rail  the nearest train station is kathgodam, which has good connectivity with most of the important places.
