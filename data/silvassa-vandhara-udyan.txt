vandhara udyan
a scenic garden with hexagon-shaped lawns, vandhara udyan is dotted with bright bougainvillea blooms and is divided by dark green hedges of casuarinas. the garden is located across river daman ganga and offers a peaceful and tranquil environment to visitors. the well-maintained garden is filled with flower enclosures and lush green vegetation. strolling through the breezy boulevards of the garden is a refreshing experience. the garden has ornate seating spaces where you can relax and unwind in natural surroundings. the gurgling sound of the river makes for a soothing melody. vandhara udyan remains open for visitors from 6 am to 12.30 pm and then from 4 pm to 8.30 pm.
