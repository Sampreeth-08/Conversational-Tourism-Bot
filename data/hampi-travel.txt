hampi
Travel Air Road Train
by air the nearest airport from hampi is hubli (about 167 km). other airports are at  belagavi (271 km) and bengaluru (about 348 km).
by road  hampi is connected by good motorable roads to major cities in india.
by rail the nearest railway station is hospet located about 13 km away from hampi. it connects hampi to bengaluru and hubli.
