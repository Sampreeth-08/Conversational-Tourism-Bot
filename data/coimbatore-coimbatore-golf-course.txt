coimbatore golf course
more than 25 years old, this golf course is a part of the coimbatore golf club, which is a members-only club. it is an 18-hole golf course. the first nine holes of the course were laid in 1985 and the next nine were laid 5 years later in 1990. it was also the first privately-owned golf club started after world war ii.
