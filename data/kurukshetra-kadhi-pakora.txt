kadhi pakora
a local staple, any visit to kurukshetra is incomplete without savouring this spicy and tangy stew made with lentil flour and curd. to make this, gram flour pakodas (fritters) filled with onions are dipped in a creamy curry made from sour yoghurt. kadhi pakora is best served with plain rice or flatbread and can be eaten both for lunch and dinner.
