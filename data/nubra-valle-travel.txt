nubra-valle
Travel Air Road Train
by air  the closest airport is bakula rimpoche airport at leh. it is one of the highest commercial airstrips in the world.
by road nubra valley is well-connected to leh and one has to take one of the highest motorable passes in the world, khardung la pass, to reach here.
rail  the nearest railhead is at jammu tawi, around 750 km away. it is well-connected to other cities.
