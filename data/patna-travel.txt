patna
Travel Air Road Train
by air with over 2,500 years of history, patna is a vibrant hub of arts, culture, literature and heritage.
by road patna can be reached via regular buses operated by bstdc and other private agencies from cities like bodhgaya (about 111km), bhagalpur (about 236km), allahabad (about 390km), varanasi (about 282km) and ranchi (about 323km).
rail patna has multiple railway stations like danapur (dnr), patna junction (pnbe), rajendra nagar bihar (rjpb), patna saheb (pnc), pothahi (pft) that are connected to major indian cities like mumbai, new delhi and kolkata by regular train services.
